About Hindu Gods

Hinduism is one of the oldest religions in the world, with a rich mythology that includes a vast array of gods and goddesses. Each deity has its own unique attributes, stories, and significance.

The Trimurti, consisting of Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva, represents the three fundamental aspects of the divine: creation, preservation, and destruction. Alongside them, goddesses like Lakshmi, Saraswati, and Parvati play crucial roles in the cosmic order.
